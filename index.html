Welcome

This website compares Japan and Nigeria on key demographic and infrastructure indicators. Explore the data, charts, maps, and future predictions below.

Japan: Aging Superpower

Japan has one of the world's lowest birth rates (6 per 1,000) and highest life expectancies, leading to an aging population. With 92% urbanization, it's highly developed but faces rural shrinkage.

Nigeria: Youthful Giant

Nigeria's population is booming with a birth rate of 33 per 1,000, but infrastructure lags. Only 55% urbanized, it struggles with access to electricity (61%) and healthcare.

Key Contrast

Japan's population is declining, while Nigeria's is growing rapidly. This affects everything from urban planning to economic policies.
